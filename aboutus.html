About Our Service & Ministry
NFP Phone APP System is completely natural.

It is simply a method of recognizing the easily
observable, visable signs of a woman's body.

Then, by applying simple guidelines, these
observable signs can be tracked with the
phone APP.

We suggest that a couple receives training prior
to using the phone APP, but it is certainly not
necessary. Procedures for using the APP are  
right on your phone.

The Catholic Fertility Counseling Center is
available to assist you if you are using one of
these APPS:
-- Family of the Americas (English & Spanish)
-- Billings
-- Sympto-thermal
